Data Abstraction-Attributes and Types

Global Country Data

Type: Tabular data

Attributes:

  • State (string): The name of the states.
  • Population (integer): Total population of the states.
  • Sex Ratio (float): The ratio of males to females in the population.
  • Literacy Rate (float): Percentage of the population that is literate.
  • Urban Population (float): Percentage of the population living in urban areas.
  • Rural Population (float): Percentage of the population living in rural areas.

Agricultural Data

Type: Tabular data

Attributes:

  • Crop Area (float): Area of Land using for growing different crops
  • Production (float): Total production of crops.
  • Yield (float): Yield of crops per unit area.
  • State (string): The name of the state.
  • Detailed Description:
  • This dataset contains structured data related to agriculture, including crop area, production quantity, and yield for different states. It provides insights into agricultural productivity and land usage patterns across regions.
